Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about LocalDeals Hub

How much does it cost for store owners to join?

We offer three pricing plans: Starter ($10/month), Professional ($20/month), and Enterprise ($30/month). Each plan includes different features to match your business needs. There's no setup fee, and you can cancel anytime.

Is it free for customers to use?

Yes! LocalDeals Hub is completely free for customers. You can browse deals, save favorites, and get directions to stores without any cost. We make money by charging a small monthly fee to store owners who use our platform.

How do I redeem deals in-store?

Each deal includes a QR code that you can show at checkout. Store staff will scan the code to apply your discount. Some deals may require mentioning the promotion or showing the deal on your phone.

What types of stores can join?

We welcome all types of local retail businesses including clothing boutiques, electronics stores, beauty salons, convenience stores, shoe stores, grocery stores, bookstores, and specialty shops. If you have a physical retail location, you can join!

How do you verify store locations?

We verify all store locations through multiple methods including business license verification, address confirmation, and in some cases, site visits. This ensures customers can trust that they're visiting legitimate local businesses.
